##### Business Insights

###### 1\. Sales Trend

**Observation**
Sales remained strong throughout 2024–2025 with monthly fluctuations, indicating recurring seasonal demand rather than a continuous decline.

**Data Evidence**
Monthly sales ranged from approximately **7.46 million** to **10.86 million**.

**Business Interpretation**
The business maintains stable demand, but some months consistently outperform others, suggesting seasonality or promotional effects.

**Recommended Action**
Analyze the highest-performing months to identify successful campaigns or seasonal factors that can be replicated during slower periods.

\---

###### 2\. Regional Performance

**Observation**
The South region is the strongest contributor to both sales and profit.

**Data Evidence**

* South Sales: **64.69 million**
* South Profit: **9.99 million**

**Business Interpretation**
The South market demonstrates strong customer demand and healthy profitability.

**Recommended Action**
Expand successful sales and marketing strategies from the South into lower-performing regions.

\---

###### 3\. Category Profitability

**Observation**
Technology is the most profitable product category.

**Data Evidence**
Technology generated approximately **28.04 million** in profit, significantly exceeding Furniture (**3.56 million**) and Office Supplies (**1.71 million**).

**Business Interpretation**
Technology products contribute the majority of business profitability.

**Recommended Action**
Increase investment in high-performing technology products while reviewing pricing and cost structures for lower-profit categories.

\---

###### 4\. Customer Segment Behavior

**Observation**
The Home Office segment delivers the highest sales and profit.

**Data Evidence**

* Sales: **74.50 million**
* Profit: **11.56 million**

**Business Interpretation**
Home Office customers represent the company's most valuable customer segment.

**Recommended Action**
Develop loyalty programs and targeted promotions to retain and grow this segment.

\---

###### 5\. Discount Impact

**Observation**
Average profit decreases as discount levels increase.

**Data Evidence**
Average profit per order declined from approximately **11,643** for discounts up to 10% to about **2,915** for discounts above 20%.

**Business Interpretation**
Aggressive discounting improves sales opportunities but significantly reduces profitability.

**Recommended Action**
Review discount policies and establish thresholds that maximize both revenue and profit.

\---

###### 6\. Shipping Performance

**Observation**
Same Day shipping generated the highest average profit among shipping modes.

**Data Evidence**
Average profit by shipping mode:

* Same Day: **9,102**
* Second Class: **8,261**
* Standard Class: **7,839**
* First Class: **7,364**

**Business Interpretation**
Faster shipping options may attract higher-value purchases or improve customer satisfaction.

**Recommended Action**
Evaluate whether expanding premium shipping services could increase profitability.

\---

###### 7\. Return Pattern

**Observation**
Furniture has the highest product return rate.

**Data Evidence**
Return rates:

* Furniture: **7.67%**
* Office Supplies: **3.65%**
* Technology: **3.03%**

**Business Interpretation**
Furniture products are more likely to be returned, increasing operational costs and reducing customer satisfaction.

**Recommended Action**
Investigate product quality, packaging, and shipping processes for furniture items.

\---

###### 8\. Business Risk and Opportunity

**Observation**
The business has strong profitability drivers but also faces risks from discounting and product returns.

**Data Evidence**
Technology contributes the largest share of profit, while Furniture experiences the highest return rate and higher discounts reduce average profit.

**Business Interpretation**
Growth opportunities exist in expanding profitable product lines, while operational improvements can reduce avoidable losses.

**Recommended Action**
Prioritize investment in high-margin categories and implement initiatives to reduce unnecessary discounts and product returns.
